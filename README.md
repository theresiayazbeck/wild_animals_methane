This repository includes a matlab script to compute methane emissions from wild ruminants based on the published model of Pérez-Barbería (2017) and global mammal biomass and population estimation in Greenspoon et al. (2023) and Greenspoon et al. (2025). The repository includes as well the code's output as csv files.

References

Pérez-Barbería, F. J. (2017). Scaling methane emissions in ruminants and global estimates in wild populations. Science of the Total Environment, 579, 1572–1580. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scitotenv.2016.11.175

Greenspoon, L., Krieger, E., Sender, R., Rosenberg, Y., Bar-On, Y. M., Moran, U., Antman, T., Meiri, S., Roll, U., Noor, E., & Milo, R. (2023). The global biomass of wild mammals. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America, 120(10). https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2204892120

Greenspoon, L., Ramot, N., Moran, U., Roll, U., Phillips, R., Noor, E., & Milo, R. (2025). The global biomass of mammals since 1850. Nature Communications , 16(1). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-63888-z
